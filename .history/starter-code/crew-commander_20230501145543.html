Douglas Gerald Hurley is an American engineer, former Marine Corps pilot and former NASA astronaut. He launched into space for the third time as commander of Crew Dragon Demo-2.

Anousheh Ansari

Anousheh Ansari is an Iranian-American engineer and co-founder of Prodea Systems. She is the first Iranian and the first self-funded woman to travel to space.

Mark Shuttleworth

Mark Shuttleworth is a South African entrepreneur and philanthropist. He is the founder of Canonical Ltd. and the Ubuntu operating system. He traveled to space as a spaceflight participant.

Victor Glover

Victor Glover is an American astronaut and a former naval aviator. He is a crew member of the Crew-1 mission to the International Space Station, which launched in November 2020.
